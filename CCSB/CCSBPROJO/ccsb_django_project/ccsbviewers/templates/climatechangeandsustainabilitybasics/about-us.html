Who Are We

Climate Change and Sustainability Basics (CCSB) is an organization which is being started with the aim of passing knowledge

According to WHO, by 2030 climate change could impact the earth by causing the following; warmer water and flooding to increase exposure to diseases in drinking and recreational water, we face up to 250,000 deaths as a result of diseases like malaria, malnutrition, diarrhea and heat stress. Also vector diseases like dengue virus will increase with more humidity and heat.

There will be a 4o temperature rise which will disrupt precipitation patterns and the frequency and intensity of some of extreme weather events.

Hunger and famine will increase as food production is destabilized by drought. Pollution and pollen seasons will increase leading to more allergies and asthma which will lead to deaths of up to 7million people due to air pollution.

All these aspects to be addressed will cost the world approximately $2-4Billion Climate change is as a result of several aspects mostly contributed by human beings.

OBJECTIVES

1. Climate change knowledge dissemination to the Kenyan society.

2. Collaborating with educational, research and industrial organizations in climate change management strategies (adaptation, mitigation and residual damages).

3. Co-generation of sustainable actions that adaptively builds community resilience to climate change impacts.
